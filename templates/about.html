ABOUT WEBSITE

This website is for anyone who is interested in learning Django, anyone in the process of learning, done learning or curious about Django. I want to use this platform to encourage people to learn Django, talk about things I experienced when learning and practicing Django, share good tips on learning Django and any programming language in general. I will post articles here at least once a week and they are free for everyone to read. People who would like to leave comments on the articles will have to be users and if anyone wants a notification for new articles, they would have to be users too. I hope this website becomes useful to many people.
